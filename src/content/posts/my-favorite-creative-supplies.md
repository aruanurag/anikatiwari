Some supplies make me want to start a project immediately.

Right now, my favorites are smooth sketch paper, watercolor sets with lots of blue and peach shades, a notebook with thick pages, and tiny measuring spoons for baking. I also love having colorful sticky notes nearby because they are helpful for story ideas and recipe reminders.

I think good supplies do not have to be fancy. They just have to make you excited to begin.
