These parfaits are easy to make and feel special enough for guests.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups vanilla yogurt
- 2 cups sliced strawberries
- 1 cup granola
- 2 tablespoons honey
- A few mint leaves for the top

## How to make them

1. Spoon a little yogurt into each glass or jar.
2. Add a layer of strawberries and a sprinkle of granola.
3. Repeat the layers until the cups are full.
4. Drizzle honey over the top and finish with mint.

The best part is seeing the pink, white, and gold layers through the glass before everyone digs in.
