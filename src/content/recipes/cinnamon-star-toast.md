This recipe turns a simple breakfast into something that looks a little bit celebratory.

## Ingredients

- 4 slices of bread
- 2 tablespoons softened butter
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- Banana slices or berries for topping

## How to make it

1. Toast the bread until golden.
2. Spread a thin layer of butter while it is still warm.
3. Mix the sugar and cinnamon together and sprinkle it over the toast.
4. Add fruit on top, arranging a few pieces into star shapes if you like.

It is quick, warm, and cheerful, which is exactly what a breakfast should be.
