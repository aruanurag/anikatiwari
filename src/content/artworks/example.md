This artwork started with a simple question: what if flowers were light enough to float?

The painting mixes soft watercolor washes with colored-pencil details to create a garden that feels airy, cheerful, and slightly magical. The balloons are painted in different sizes so the path looks busy and playful, almost like the whole scene is taking a gentle breath.

My favorite part is the line of tiny leaves near the bottom edge because they help ground the painting while everything above them feels ready to drift upward.
